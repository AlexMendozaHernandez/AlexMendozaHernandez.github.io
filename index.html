About Me

I am currently studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of North Florida. I enjoy learning about robotics, computer programming and graphic design. I also enjoy using my knowledge on these topics to educate students who have a passion for them. I have been teaching STEM-based curriculum since 2021 and continue to learn more as I teach.

Although I have a passion to educate students, I also have a passion for political accountability. As an Undergraduate Student at the Univeristy of North Florida, I understand the importance of representation and informational transparency. Because of these strong beliefs, I have served as a Senator in the UNF Student Government since Fall of 2022 and continue to serve as the Senate President Pro-Tempore.
